# Signature Dishes Configuration Guide

## Overview

The Signature Dishes feature allows you to showcase 3 special dishes from your menu on the homepage. This guide will walk you through configuring them.

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Add Menu Items

Before you can select signature dishes, you need to have menu items in your system.

1. Log in to the Admin Panel
2. Navigate to the **Menu** tab
3. Click **Add Menu Item**
4. Fill in the dish details:
   - Name
   - Description
   - Price
   - Category
   - Image URL
   - Allergens (optional)
5. Click **Save**

Repeat this process to add multiple dishes.

---

### Step 2: Configure Signature Dishes

1. In the Admin Panel, navigate to the **General** tab
2. Scroll to the **Our Signature Dishes** section
3. You'll see 3 dropdown menus labeled:
   - Signature Dish 1
   - Signature Dish 2
   - Signature Dish 3

4. For each dropdown:
   - Click to open the menu
   - Browse through your available dishes
   - Select the dish you want to feature
   - A preview will appear below showing:
     - Dish image
     - Name
     - Description
     - Price

5. **Important Notes**:
   - You cannot select the same dish twice
   - Already selected dishes will be disabled in other dropdowns
   - You can change your selection at any time

6. Once you've selected your 3 dishes, click **Save Signature Dishes**

---

### Step 3: Verify on Homepage

1. Navigate to the homepage of your website
2. Scroll to the **Our Signature Dishes** section
3. Your selected dishes should now be displayed with:
   - High-quality images
   - Dish names
   - Descriptions
   - Prices

---

## Tips for Choosing Signature Dishes

1. **Visual Appeal**: Choose dishes with attractive, high-quality images
2. **Variety**: Select dishes from different categories (e.g., starter, main, dessert)
3. **Popularity**: Feature your best-selling or most unique dishes
4. **Seasonal**: Update regularly to reflect seasonal offerings
5. **Price Range**: Consider showcasing dishes at different price points

---

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Signature dishes not showing on homepage

**Solutions**:
- Ensure you clicked "Save Signature Dishes" after selecting
- Refresh the homepage (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
- Check that the selected dishes have valid images
- Verify the dishes still exist in your menu

### Problem: Can't select a dish

**Solutions**:
- Check if the dish is already selected in another slot
- Ensure you have menu items added
- Try refreshing the admin panel

### Problem: Preview not showing

**Solutions**:
- Verify the dish has an image URL
- Check that the image URL is valid and accessible
- Try selecting a different dish

---

## Default Behavior

If you haven't configured signature dishes yet, the homepage will display default placeholder dishes. Once you configure your own dishes, they will replace the defaults.

---

## Best Practices

1. **Update Regularly**: Keep your signature dishes fresh by updating them monthly or seasonally
2. **High-Quality Images**: Use professional photos (minimum 800x600px)
3. **Compelling Descriptions**: Write appetizing descriptions that highlight what makes each dish special
4. **Monitor Performance**: Track which dishes get the most orders after being featured
5. **Test on Mobile**: Always check how your dishes look on mobile devices
