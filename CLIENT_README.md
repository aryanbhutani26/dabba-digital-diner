### Managing Your Menu

#### Adding a New Dish:
1. Log in to your **Admin Panel**
2. Click on the **Menu** tab
3. Click **"Add Menu Item"** button
4. Fill in the details:
   - **Name**: e.g., "Chicken Tikka Masala"
   - **Description**: Brief description of the dish
   - **Price**: In pounds (Â£)
   - **Category**: Choose from Mains, Lunch, Drinks, or Desserts

   - **Allergens**: List any allergens (comma-separated)
   - **Image**: Upload a photo of the dish
5. Click **"Create Item"**

#### Editing a Dish:
1. Go to **Admin Panel** â†’ **Menu** tab
2. Find the dish you want to edit

#### Deleting a Dish:
1. Go to **Admin Panel** â†’ **Menu** tab
2. Find the dish you want to remove
3. Click the **Delete** button (trash icon)
4. Confirm deletion

### Controlling the Lunch Menu

#### To Disable Lunch Service:
1. Log in to your **Admin Panel**
2. Click on the **General Settings** tab
3. Scroll to **"Lunch Menu Availability"**
4. Toggle the switch to **OFF**
5. Done! Customers will now see that lunch is unavailable

#### To Enable Lunch Service:
1. Follow the same steps above
2. Toggle the switch to **ON**
3. Customers can now order from the lunch menu

**When to Use This:**
- When you stop serving lunch for the day
- During holidays when lunch is not available
- When you want to temporarily disable lunch orders

## ðŸŽ¯ Best Practices

### Menu Organization Tips:

1. **Use Subcategories Wisely**:
   - Group similar items together
   - Examples: "Tandoori Items", "Rice", "Curries", "Breads"
   - Makes it easier for customers to find what they want

2. **Keep Descriptions Clear**:
   - Brief but descriptive
   - Mention key ingredients
   - Highlight what makes it special

3. **Always List Allergens**:
   - Important for customer safety
   - Common allergens: Dairy, Nuts, Gluten, Shellfish, Eggs

4. **Use High-Quality Images**:
   - Clear, well-lit photos
   - Show the actual dish
   - Makes items more appealing

### Lunch Menu Management:

1. **Daily Routine**:
   - Enable lunch menu in the morning
   - Disable when lunch service ends
   - Can be done in seconds from your phone

2. **Special Occasions**:
   - Disable on holidays if not serving lunch
   - Enable for special lunch events
   - Update anytime without technical help

## ðŸ†˜ Common Questions

### Q: Can I add my own subcategories?
**A:** Yes! When adding a menu item, you can select "+ Add New Subcategory" and create your own.

### Q: What happens to orders when I disable lunch?
**A:** Customers cannot place new lunch orders, but existing orders are not affected.

### Q: Can I disable other categories like Drinks or Desserts?
**A:** Currently, only the lunch menu has a toggle. If you need this for other categories, let us know!

### Q: How do I change the logo?
**A:** Contact your developer to replace the logo file. The logo should be square (at least 200x200 pixels).

### Q: Can customers still see lunch items when disabled?
**A:** Yes, they can see the items but they're grayed out and cannot be ordered. This helps customers know what's available during lunch hours.

### Q: Does the lunch toggle work on mobile?
**A:** Yes! You can enable/disable lunch from any device - phone, tablet, or computer.
